Hardware setup
**************

 MMA8652 Breakout ------------ Arduino
 3.3V ------------------------ 3.3V
 SDA ------------------------- Analog 4
 SCL ------------------------- Analog 5
 INT2 ------------------------ Digital 5
 INT1 ------------------------ Digital 4
 GND ------------------------- GND
 
 SDA and SCL should have external pull-up resistors (to 3.3V).
 10k resistors are on the FRDM-FXS-MULTI breakout board.
